Basys 3 port --> OV7670 port
M18,JC2      --> XCLK
A15,JB7      --> D7
A14,JB1,     --> D6
C16,JB10     --> D5
B16,JB4      --> D4
C15,JB9      --> D3
B15,JB3      --> D2
A17,JB8      --> D1
A16,JB2      --> D0
M19,JC8      --> HREF
N17,JC3      --> PCLK
R18,JC10     --> SIOC
P18,JC4      --> SIOD
P17,JC9      --> VSYNC
PWR,JC6      --> 3v3
PWR,JC12     --> RESET
GND,JC5      --> GND
GND,JC11     --> PWDN
